How to make a ticket

  1. Click Join Discord and accept the invite. If you don’t have Discord yet, create a free account when prompted.
  2. Read the server rules and verify in the verify-here channel.
  3. To make a ticket, navigate to the create-a-ticket channel and that will prompt you to Direct Message @THE COLLECTIVE SUPPORT
  4. In your new ticket, you will be greeted with an initial message - It is important to read and understand this before proceeding.
  5. Describe what you are looking for: Chains, Weapons ect. The more detail, the faster we can reply.
  6. Wait for Staff reply in your ticket — We will respond as soon as we can.
